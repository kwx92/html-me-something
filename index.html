Try our famous cream cheese icing at home!
  • 8oz-Cream cheese
  • 1/2C-Butter (1 stick)
  • 2-3C-Powdered sugar
  • 1T-Vanilla extract
  1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and butter together until smoothe.
  2. Add in vanilla extract, beat until smoothe.
  3. Slowly (about 1 cup at a time) add powdered sugar.
  4. Add until consistency/taste is as desired.
  5. Chill and enjoy!
